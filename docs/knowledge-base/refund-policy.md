# Refund policy — Sericia

## The short version

- **Unopened and we made a mistake** (wrong item, damaged in transit, undelivered): full refund or replacement, your choice.
- **Opened and you don't like it**: we can't resell food once opened, so no refund — but please tell us why, it shapes future sourcing.
- **Unopened and you changed your mind**: return allowed within 14 days of delivery at your shipping cost, less a 10% restocking fee.

## The detail

### When we refund in full

1. **Damaged in transit**
   - Email <contact@sericia.com> within 7 days of delivery with photos of the parcel + contents.
   - We open the EMS insurance claim and refund or replace immediately — we don't wait for Japan Post.

2. **Wrong item delivered**
   - Email within 14 days with photos.
   - We send the correct drop (if still in stock) or refund, and cover the return shipping if you want to send the wrong one back (usually we tell you to keep it — postage from the US to Japan is often more than the drop value).

3. **Parcel lost in transit**
   - If tracking shows no movement for 10+ business days after dispatch, email us.
   - We open the claim with Japan Post and, regardless of the claim outcome, refund or reship within 48 hours.

4. **Expired on arrival**
   - Every drop has a guaranteed minimum shelf life on arrival (see [ingredients.md](./ingredients.md)).
   - If your drop arrives with less shelf life than the minimum, full refund.

### When we refund partially (restocking fee)

- **Unopened, within 14 days, you changed your mind**: return at your own shipping cost. We inspect on arrival in Kyoto, and if the seal is intact and the best-before is still 30+ days away, we refund the item price minus a 10% restocking fee.
- Shipping fees you paid are non-refundable for changed-mind returns.

### When we can't refund

- **Opened food items.** Food safety law in Japan (and most destinations) prohibits reselling opened food. Once a tin of sencha or a jar of miso is open, it's yours.
- **Flavor preference.** Japanese craft food is strongly seasonal and regional — a first-flush sencha tastes nothing like a standard supermarket green tea. We write drop pages to set expectations; if a flavor isn't what you imagined, we read feedback but can't refund on taste alone.
- **More than 30 days after delivery.** Our return window is 14 days for changed-mind returns, 7 days for damaged/wrong-item claims. After 30 days we assume the drop was enjoyed.
- **Customs refused.** If your country's customs authority refuses the parcel (for non-compliant ingredients or import restrictions), we'll refund the item price if the parcel returns to Kyoto — shipping is non-refundable.

## How refunds land

- **Credit card (Crossmint)**: refunds go back to the originating card within 5–10 business days. You'll see the reversal on your statement; we don't issue a separate receipt.
- **Crypto (USDC, ETH)**: we refund the same token to your original sending address. Processed within 2 business days.

## How to start a refund

Email <contact@sericia.com> with:

1. Your order ID (format: 8-character hex prefix from your order confirmation)
2. Photos if damage or wrong-item
3. One sentence on what happened

A human reads every email. Expect a first reply within 24 hours (JST business hours — Mon–Fri, 9:00–18:00 Japan time). We don't route refund requests through the chatbot.

## Chargebacks

If you dispute a Sericia charge with your card issuer before contacting us, the issuer freezes the funds and we can't refund you directly. Please email first — we're small and we refund fast.
