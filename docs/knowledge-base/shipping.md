# Shipping — Sericia

## How we ship

Every Sericia drop ships from Kyoto, Japan by **EMS (Express Mail Service)** operated by Japan Post. EMS is tracked end-to-end and is the fastest insured option for food products leaving Japan.

## How long it takes

| Destination | Typical transit |
|---|---|
| Asia-Pacific (KR, TW, HK, SG, AU) | 2–4 business days |
| United States | 3–6 business days |
| Europe / UK | 4–7 business days |
| Middle East | 5–8 business days |
| Rest of world | 6–10 business days |

Transit starts after we dispatch, which is **within 48 hours of payment confirmation** for stocked drops. Pre-order drops ship on the date listed on the drop page.

## Shipping cost

Shipping is weight-based, calculated at checkout from the Japan Post EMS table:

| Parcel weight | US/EU | Asia-Pacific |
|---|---|---|
| ≤ 100 g | ¥1,350 | ¥900 |
| ≤ 250 g | ¥1,750 | ¥1,180 |
| ≤ 500 g | ¥2,150 | ¥1,450 |
| ≤ 1 kg | ¥3,100 | ¥2,100 |
| ≤ 2 kg | ¥4,900 | ¥3,300 |

Prices shown at checkout are converted to the currency of the region you shop from.

## Tracking

Your tracking number arrives by email within 24 hours of dispatch. Track it at any of:

- **Japan Post** (most detailed): <https://trackings.post.japanpost.jp/services/srv/search/?locale=en>
- **USPS** (for US destinations after handoff): <https://tools.usps.com/go/TrackConfirmAction_input>
- **Royal Mail / local post** (EU/UK/others): once the parcel lands in-country

## Customs and duties

You are responsible for any customs duties or import taxes assessed by your country's customs authority. Sericia declares all parcels at their actual retail value (required by Japanese export law) and we cannot mark parcels as gifts or under-declare them.

Most Sericia drops are under the **de minimis threshold** (the duty-free allowance) for the following destinations:

| Country | De minimis (roughly) |
|---|---|
| United States | USD 800 |
| United Kingdom | GBP 135 |
| EU | EUR 150 |
| Australia | AUD 1,000 |
| Japan (returns) | JPY 10,000 |

Orders over the threshold may incur a small import VAT / duty. If you're unsure, contact <contact@sericia.com> with the drop you're eyeing and we'll walk through it.

## Damaged or lost parcels

EMS includes automatic insurance up to ¥20,000 (about USD 130) per parcel. If your drop arrives damaged or doesn't arrive within the expected window, email <contact@sericia.com> with:

1. Your order ID
2. Photos of the parcel + contents (for damage)
3. Tracking screenshot showing no movement (for lost)

We will open the claim with Japan Post and send a replacement drop or full refund — your choice.

## Addresses we can't ship to

- PO Boxes in the US (EMS requires a street address)
- Military APO/FPO addresses (contact us first — sometimes possible)
- Sanctioned destinations per Japanese export law (refer to <https://www.meti.go.jp/policy/anpo/> for the current list)
